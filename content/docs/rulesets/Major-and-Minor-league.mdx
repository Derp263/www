---
title: Major and Minor League
description: The Major and Minor League ruleset is used for Major and Minor League Balatro games. 
---

## Major League Ruleset
This ruleset features no modifications to base game, except for boss blinds being pvp blinds. 
The timer is set to **180** seconds and this ruleset forces you to have The Order disabled.

## Minor League Ruleset
Just like Major League, This ruleset features no modifications to base game, except for boss blinds being pvp blinds. 
The timer is set to **180** seconds. However, this ruleset forces you to have The Order enabled.
